The Best Dad Jokes

Why do dads love dad jokes so much?

Because they're 'pun'-damental to their sense of humor!

Bad day at work? Failed that class? Girlfriend break up with you? We all need a pick-me-up once in awhile. Let us do that for you!





Jokes of the Week:

  1. Hungry?
    • Why did the tomato turn red? Because it saw the salad dressing!
    • Why don't we trust tacos? They tend to spill the beans!
  2. Sad?
    • Feeling a bit down? Just remember, even a broken pencil can still have a point!
    • Feeling blue? Just remember, a day without sunshine is like, you know, night!
  3. Stressed?
    • Just remember, a good book can be uplifting, especially if you're standing on it!
    • Feeling stressed? Just remember, an apple a day keeps the doctor away, but if the doctor is cute, forget the fruit!
